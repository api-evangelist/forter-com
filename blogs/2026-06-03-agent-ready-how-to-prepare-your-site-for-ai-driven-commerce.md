AI agents embedded in ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and similar platforms are actively browsing and attempting purchases on behalf of users, and sites not optimized for agent comprehension will be bypassed before any transaction occurs. Forter outlines five sequential layers of agent-readiness — discoverability, comprehension, trust, actionability, and experience — that merchants must address as the distinction between human-readable and agent-readable sites becomes a competitive differentiator.
